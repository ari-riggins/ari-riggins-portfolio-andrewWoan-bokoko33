About Me

01

Hi! I'm Ari, a recent graduate from Princeton University where I majored in computer science and minored in visual arts. I have deep interests in computer graphics, user experience design, animation, and XR (specifically augmented reality), as well as in problem-solving and algorithms. One of my main passions is converting data into meaningful conclusions, whether visually, interactively, or through clear statisics. I have contributed to a range of research projects from data visualization utilizing AR, to data analysis and prediction within nonlinear dynamics. I have recently completed independent research through my thesis project which uses 3D modeling and AR to investigate human perception of both physical and virtual spaces. As a detail-oriented and proactive problem solver, I am intrested in adding my unique perspective to future projects.

My Work

02

Gallery Show

Show titled "Nucleus, but like a Spiral" was held in Princeton's Hagan gallery. I created it using techniques such as projection mapping, crystalization, plasterwork, crochet, and more. I also designed the poster to promote the show. A video interview explaining the show can be found at the link.

Computational Geometry Algorithm

I coded several algorithms to calculate and visualize the equidecomposition of any two equal area polygons using Java. The goal was to show that a polygon could be cut up and restructured into any other polygon of the same area.

ResNFT Trading Platform

A Flow blockchain based NFT minting and trading platform focused on restaurant reservations. Provides a place for restaurants to list deposit reservations as NFTs and for customers to purchase these reservations and resell them as needed.

3D Mini Golf Game

Mini golf web app game designed and created in a group of three students. We used Three.js for the graphics and modeled the physics in our game.

Data Visualization Project

Self-driven research project to develop my own form of artistic data visualization by creating my own data set with information scraped from the app TikTok.

This Site

I created this site by first designing the 3D room model in Blender, then by bringing that into my JavaScript using Three.js. I chose this site design for its user responsiveness and fun layout. By following an online tutorial by Andrew Woan, I was able to code all of the functionality and even add a dark mode feature.
